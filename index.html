User Research · May 2026
Not an idea.
A validated observation.

Before any screen was designed, I interviewed real Ajo participants to understand what actually matters — and what would kill adoption of a digital version. Here's what they said.

Finding 01
Trust is personal — always
Both respondents built groups only through personal networks. No stranger would be accepted, regardless of app verification. MonieCircle is private-group only — by design.

"You are the one bringing them in. If they misbehave, it's on you."

— Respondent 1, Active Ajo Coordinator
Finding 02
The coordinator is the most stressed person
Reminders, disputes, covering shortfalls — all on one person. MonieCircle automates exactly these tasks: auto-deduction, tracking, and disbursement.

"You have to remind them — this is the due date — enforcing the boundaries. That is the most stressful part."

— Respondent 1
Finding 03
Delayed payouts are a recurring pain
Two delayed payouts experienced — both because the coordinator bridged shortfalls personally. Auto-deduction + Monie Sense early nudges + Moniepoint's nationwide agent network eliminates this.
Finding 04
The app must organise, not hold money
Both respondents rejected the idea of an app "holding" their funds. They want a facilitation layer. MonieCircle is positioned as "your Ajo, organised" — not a savings product.

"The app verifying them doesn't mean they won't withdraw and disappear."

— Respondent 2
Finding 05
Fees must be group-agreed, not imposed
One respondent rejected any platform fee outright. The other accepted it only if the group collectively agrees upfront. MonieCircle's fee model is opt-in and transparent from day one.
Finding 06
A trust safety net would close the deal
Respondent 2 said a traceability mechanism would significantly increase confidence. This directly validates Monie Sense's Ajo Trust Score feature — covered in the AI section.
The verdict:
Real problem. Conditional appetite.
Adoption will happen — if the app feels like a trusted tool for known groups, not a risky fintech product. MonieCircle is designed around every one of these conditions.
✅ Private groups only — no strangers by design
✅ App as organiser — not a bank holding your money
✅ Coordinator-first — automates the most painful tasks
✅ Group-agreed fees — no platform surprises
✅ Trust Score — behavioural safety net before joining
The Feature Suite
Four features.
One cultural moat.

Each one rooted in real Nigerian behaviour. Together, they make Moniepoint the platform where Nigerians do money — alone and together.

01 · Hero MonieCircle  — "Ajo, organised"
Your community, your turn +

What it is

A transparent, in-app rotating savings group for private circles. Admin creates a group, adds members by phone or account number, sets amount and payout rotation. Every member sees the live balance. Admin cannot hide a kobo.

Research-backed: Private-group only — because both respondents only trust people they personally know.

Why it wins

  • Automates coordinator's most painful tasks
  • Full transparency kills admin fraud
  • Auto-deduction removes the chasing
  • 36-state agent network: cash-in anywhere
  • Group-agreed fee — not silently imposed
  • Disclaimer protects Moniepoint legally

The moat

Millions of informal Ajo groups move onto a regulated, transparent platform. Every new member is a potential Moniepoint user recruited by their Ajo group. Viral by nature.

First major bank to own this
02MonieGoal Together — Mutual Savings
Same dream, shared hustle+

What it is

Two or more people set a shared savings target — a car, rent, a trip. Each contributes to one pot. All parties see the live breakdown of who contributed what and how close they are.

Why it wins

  • No equivalent in Nigerian fintech
  • Shared accountability drives completion
  • Transparency removes partner trust issues
  • Interest can accrue on the pooled balance

The moat

Captures couples, friends, and co-founders who currently manage shared goals in WhatsApp threads.

No competitor in Nigeria
03MonieVault — Mutual Control
Shared money, shared trust+

What it is

A shared wallet where any withdrawal requires approval from all parties. Interest-bearing. Multi-party approval gates before any money moves. For couples, co-founders, family pools.

Why it wins

  • Approval flow stops impulsive withdrawals
  • Interest rewards disciplined savers
  • Unique in the Nigerian fintech market

The moat

Solves saving together without fighting about spending — a deeply human problem with zero competition today.

Completely uncharted
04MoniePocket — Parent to Child
Send love, schedule it+

What it is

A parent-controlled wallet linked to a child's Moniepoint account. Parents fund it, set automatic allowance schedules, approve or decline withdrawal requests. Child gives a reason before any money moves. Parents get a weekly AI digest.

Why it wins

  • Solves the "busy parent" problem entirely
  • Scheduled allowances remove missed payments
  • Teaches children real financial responsibility
  • Nigeria's Greenlight — built for local reality

The moat

Captures students, boarders, and their parents — a new segment. Zero Nigerian fintech has this. Every busy parent will want it.

Nigeria's first family finance tool
MonieCircle · User Flow
How it works,
step by step.

Every step designed around a validated user insight. No new app. No extra signup. Just open Moniepoint.

01
Create Circle
Admin creates a private group, sets amount, date, and rotation. Members added by phone or account number only.
Research-backed: personal networks only.
02
Members Accept
Each member reviews full terms — amount, payout position, schedule — before confirming. No one joins blind.
03 · Key
Full Transparency
Live dashboard: who paid, who hasn't, balance, next payout. Admin cannot hide anything.
Research-backed: trust requires visibility.
04 · Key
Auto-Deduction
Moniepoint auto-debits on due date. Monie Sense nudges low-balance members 3 days early. Cash-in via any agent nationwide.
Research-backed: delayed payouts = #1 pain.
05
Payout Day
Funds (minus group-agreed fee) auto-transfer to that month's recipient. Zero manual action. Zero stress.
06
Cycle Repeats
Circle auto-resets. History permanently logged. Circle renews or dissolves after final payout.
